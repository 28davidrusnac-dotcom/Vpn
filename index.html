What is a VPN?

A VPN, or a Virtual Private Network, is a service that creates an encrypted tunnel for your internet traffic. It protects your data, secures your connection, and masks your IP address, ensuring no hackers will be able to intercept your internet presence. How NOVA interacts with All Four Layers of the Internet

1. Application Layer Encrypts all your data before it leaves your device. 2 .Transport Layer: It takes encrypted data and sends it through an encrypted network, which makes it harder for hackers to intercept your connection 3. Internet Layer It replaces your IP address with one from a NovaVPN server. 4. Network access This is where a physical connection happens. NovaVpn doesn't change the connection physically; it just wraps your data in an encrypted tunnel. Even if a hacker were to intercept the connection, all they would see is encrypted noise.
